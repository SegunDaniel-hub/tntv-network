# TNTV Network Website Documentation

## Overview
TNTV Network is a news website where users can browse, read, and share news articles across various categories.

---

## For Visitors

### Browsing the Homepage
- The homepage displays a **hero slider** featuring the latest or featured news articles
- Below the slider, you'll find **Featured Stories** and recent news articles
- Use the **navigation menu** at the top to browse by category

### Reading Articles
1. Click on any article card to open the full article
2. Articles display the title, author, publication date, and full content
3. Use the **"Back to Home"** link to return to the homepage

### Browsing by Category
1. Click on a category name in the navigation menu (e.g., Politics, Sports, Entertainment)
2. View all published articles within that category
3. Categories help organize news by topic

### Searching for News
1. Use the **search icon** in the header
2. Enter keywords to find articles by title, content, or author
3. Results will display matching published articles

### Sharing Articles
When viewing an article, you can share it via:
- **Facebook** - Share to your Facebook feed
- **Twitter/X** - Tweet the article
- **Copy Link** - Copy the article URL to clipboard

When shared on social media, the article's image, title, and excerpt will appear as a preview.

## For Administrators

### Accessing the Admin Dashboard
1. Navigate directly to `/admin/login` in your browser
2. Sign in with your admin email and password
3. If you don't have an account, use the **Sign Up** tab to create one
   - Note: New accounts require admin role assignment to access the dashboard

### Managing Articles

#### Creating a New Article
1. In the admin dashboard, click **"Create New Article"**
2. Fill in the required fields:
   - **Title** - The headline of your article
   - **Author** - The article author's name
   - **Category** - Select from available categories
   - **Content** - The full article text
   - **Excerpt** - A brief summary (shown in previews)
   - **Image** - Upload an image for the article
3. Toggle options:
   - **Featured** - Mark as a featured story (appears in hero slider)
   - **Published** - Make the article visible to the public
4. Click **"Create Article"** to save

#### Editing an Article
1. Find the article in your dashboard list
2. Click the **Edit** button (pencil icon)
3. Modify any fields as needed
4. Click **"Update Article"** to save changes

#### Deleting an Article
1. Find the article in your dashboard list
2. Click the **Delete** button (trash icon)
3. Confirm the deletion when prompted

#### Managing Featured Status
- Toggle the **star icon** next to any article to feature/unfeature it
- Featured articles appear in the homepage hero slider

#### Publishing/Unpublishing
- Use the **Published** toggle when editing to control visibility
- Unpublished articles are only visible in the admin dashboard
